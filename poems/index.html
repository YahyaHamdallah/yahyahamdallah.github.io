familiar

I lie where we sat
tracing the space beside me
you smelled sweet
and asked what I meant
I could not explain
how familiar you felt to me
so I stay here just
to feel close to you.
careful not to forget.

i love you

you say,

أحبّك

and I sink again.

the words
returned only to me.

set carefully
between what is,
and what never arrives.

kept there
until it aches.

waiting to be answered.
while you search
for something beyond me.

Can’t Hold On

I felt it.

but cannot

keep it.

held a place

for something

that never arrived.

waiting

for it to choose me.

to become real.

but I cannot hold on

to nothing.

still,

somehow,

I am here.

Untitled

his body arrives

before he does.

darting his eyes

across their face.

as if something there

might pull them closer.

following their breath

careful not to lose it.

he waits for the moment

it might open.

unguarded.

he almost gives,

wanting to let it show.

he leans into it.

just enough.

as the room closes in,

eyes settle where they shouldn’t.

voices shift beneath the noise.

the room tightens.

he pulls back.

still,

their breathing holds.

close enough

to feel it

pass between them.

nothing is said

but something shared.

not yet gone.

it holds there.
