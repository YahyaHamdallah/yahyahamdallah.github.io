Untitled

his body arrives

before he does.

darting his eyes

across their face.

as if something there

might pull them closer.

following their breath

careful not to lose it.

he waits for the moment

it might open.

unguarded.

he almost gives,

wanting to let it show.

he leans into it.

just enough.

as the room closes in,

eyes settle where they shouldn’t.

voices shift beneath the noise.

the room tightens.

he pulls back.

still,

their breathing holds.

close enough

to feel it

pass between them.

nothing is said

but something shared.

not yet gone.

it holds there.
